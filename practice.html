BEYOND OF THE MIST

Her name is Lili, the daughter of a 300-year-old scientist. In front of her eyes, this mechanical world keeps turning, but her mind can never truly adapt to it.
Her father used to tell her many things related to science, like light, dimension, and time. She always pondered over those topics—

  • Why are we all moving in the same way?
  • Are we really three-dimensional?
  • If we can understand the third dimension, then what is the fourth dimension?
  • Are we just a part of this world, or is someone controlling us?
  • Is there something beyond this fog?

Lili's friends think she is crazy, but these questions keep revolving in her mind.

Every night, when the entire city falls asleep, Ara sits by the window, staring into the fog. She can feel that something exists beyond it... but what?
